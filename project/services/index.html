Cleaning Solution: Services

Tile and Grout Cleaning

Our cleaning services help keep tiles and grout spotless, enhancing the look of your home or business while promoting a healthier environment. Cleaning not only removes visible dirt but also eliminates allergens, germs, and bacteria.

Clean tiles also maintain their grip, reducing the risk of slips and falls at home. Our professional cleaning services complement your regular upkeep, ensuring your tiles and grout stay shining for longer.

Air Duct Cleaning

Professional air duct cleaning is essential when vents accumulate dust, allergens, and other contaminants over time. These airborne particles can settle inside the ductwork, affecting air quality and circulation.

Cleaning air ducts is also a critical part of recovery and restoration after events like fires, where smoke and soot can become trapped in the ventilation system. If not properly removed, these pollutants can spread through the HVAC system, impacting the air you breathe. Contact us today for a free estimate.

Floor Stripping and Waxing

At Cleaning Solution, we believe that floor stripping and waxing are essential for maintaining a clean and professional-looking facility. Proper maintenance helps preserve the beauty and longevity of your floors.

High-traffic areas tend to accumulate dirt and grime, which can dull the appearance of even the most stunning floors. With high-quality stripping and waxing, your flooring will stay clean, glossy, and visually appealing for years to come. Contact us today for a free estimate.

Floor Cleaning

Carpet is a popular flooring choice for conference rooms, hallways, offices, hotel rooms, reception areas, and other hospitality spaces. Available in a variety of colors and designs, carpet not only enhances your workspace’s appearance but also helps filter the air by trapping dirt, dust, and allergens, contributing to a healthier indoor environment.

However, without proper commercial carpet cleaning, carpets can accumulate dirt and lose their original look. Protect your investment with professional carpet cleaning services from Cleaning Solution and keep your carpets fresh and well-maintained.

Water Damage Cleanup Services

From leaking faucets and broken water heaters to severe flooding, water damage is one of the most common and destructive issues homeowners can encounter.

If not addressed promptly, excess water can create electrical hazards, cause costly damage to your home’s structure and furnishings, and even lead to hazardous mold growth. When disaster strikes, trust the water damage restoration specialists at Cleaning Solution. With just one call, we’ll be there to guide you through the process and resolve the issue quickly and efficiently.
