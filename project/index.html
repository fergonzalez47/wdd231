Site Purpose:

The website will serve as a platform for customers interested in professional home and office cleaning services. It will provide detailed information on services offered, pricing options(optional) and allow users to schedule appointments online. It will also include a contact section for direct inquiries and a testimonials page to reinforce the credibility of the business (in the future).


  • How can I book a cleaning service for my office or home?

    I will respond with a clear process for how users can schedule a service using the online booking form or Phone.

  • What type of products and techniques do you use to clean furniture and floors?

    Information will be included on the cleaning methods used, highlighting ecological or specialized practices in the care of different materials.

  • How much does a cleaning service cost for a 3-bedroom house?

    A pricing table or form will be provided to obtain a custom quote based on the size of the space and services required.

  • Do you offer deep cleaning services or just regular maintenance?

    The website will explain the difference between both services and the options available, including deep cleaning for moves, post-construction, specialized sanitation, etc.

  • Do you offer recurring cleaning plans for offices or businesses?

    Scheduled cleaning packages for offices and businesses will be described, including discounts for monthly subscriptions
